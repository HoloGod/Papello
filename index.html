Our Menu

Papellung

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside! Enjoy these golden skewers of egg and flour batter, perfectly fried and topped with a drizzle of savory sauce. Each bite is a delightful combination of rich egg flavor wrapped in a crunchy coating, making it a light and satisfying snack for any time of day. Served warm on a skewer for easy munching.

Papellon

The golden-yellow custard inside the crisp, baked tart shells has a smooth texture, with a glossy surface that suggests a rich and luscious flavor. The simple ingredients combine to create a rich custardy tart that would be soft and creamy inside, contrasted by the firm, buttery crust. Perfect for those who enjoy indulgent, sweet treats!

Es Papeto

The vibrant red hue of the syrup adds sweetness and a tropical coconut-pandan aroma. The translucent pieces of Nata de Coco and matoa fruit float in the drink, making it both colorful and texturally interesting. It's likely served chilled, perfect for a sweet, fruity refreshment!
